Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 30, 2026

Overview

Chain Day ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

Account Information

When you create an account, we collect your email address for authentication purposes. We use Clerk for secure authentication.

Habit Data

We store your habit names, completion records, and streak data to provide the core functionality of the app. This data is stored securely using Convex.

Usage Analytics

We collect anonymized usage data to improve the app experience. This does not include any personal habit information.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the app functionality
  • To sync your data across devices
  • To send habit reminders (if enabled)
  • To process subscription payments via RevenueCat
  • To improve and optimize the app

Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored securely using industry-standard encryption. We use trusted third-party services:

  • Convex - Secure database for habit data
  • Clerk - Authentication and user management
  • RevenueCat - Subscription management

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We only share data with the third-party services mentioned above, which are necessary for the app to function.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export your habit data
  • Opt out of marketing communications

Children's Privacy

Chain Day is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
